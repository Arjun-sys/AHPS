About Health Prediction System

Advanced Health Prediction system is a system used to predict disease from the symptoms which are given by the users to the system.
The study aims to provide relief to many of the busy doctors who spent their days and life treating the patients.
This system enables the doctor/patient to know about the disease according to the symptoms using data mining techniques
